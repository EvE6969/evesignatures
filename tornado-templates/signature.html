How it works
To add signatures to the tool, simply copy and paste the results from your probes (not d-scan!) by clicking into your result window, hit ctrl-a and ctrl-c (or ctrl click individual results) and paste the content into the input field above with ctrl-v. The tool should then automatically recognize and save results. This tool can be used to share results on wormholes or signatures that you're not interested in with alliance mates, either for them to pick them up or at least preventing others from probing the same signatures over and over again.
